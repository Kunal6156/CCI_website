About CCI

Cement Corporation of India Limited (CCI) is a Government of India Enterprise under the Ministry of Steel, established in 1965. We are one of India's leading cement manufacturers, committed to delivering high-quality cement products while maintaining environmental sustainability.

With our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and decades of expertise, we have contributed significantly to India's infrastructure development and continue to be a trusted partner in the nation's growth story.
