About CCI

Building India's foundation with quality cement since 1965

Our Story

Cement Corporation of India Limited (CCI) was established in 1965 as a Public Sector Enterprise under the Ministry of Steel, Government of India. Born out of the vision to contribute to India's infrastructure development, CCI has grown to become one of the country's leading cement manufacturers.

Over the decades, we have consistently delivered high-quality cement products that have been instrumental in building India's iconic structures, highways, bridges, and residential complexes. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted partner in the nation's growth story.

Today, with a combined capacity of over 17 million tonnes per annum across our manufacturing units, CCI continues to play a pivotal role in India's construction and infrastructure sectors while maintaining our commitment to environmental sustainability and social responsibility.
